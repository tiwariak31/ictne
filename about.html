ABOUT ICTNE

ICTNE Institute was approved by the Govt.of India MSME in the year 2022 with the aim of imparting quality technical education for the students of Prayagraj and nearby areas. Since its inception, the faculty and staff have been tirelessly

OUR VISION

The vision of ICTNE Institute is to produce a society grounded in technological advancement, social justice and equality by combining knowledge, creativity, ethics and co-operation.

OUR MISSION

The mission of ICTNE Institute is the advancement of knowledge by educating students in Computer Networking Engineering Cloud Computing, Cyber Security Microsoft Server and Computer Hardware. By following a holistic and inter-disciplinary approach, we believe in producing Engineers who would serve the country and the world in the 21st century. The eminent faculty of the institute is committed in generating knowledge and disseminating it to the students, thereby nurturing them to become torch bearers of knowledge. By combining academic rig our and excitement of discovery in the subject, the faculty is committed to producing students who would become embodiments of intelligence of both theory and practice. The Institute is committed to the overall development of the students, making them fine citizens dedicated to the betterment of the society

OUR OBJECTIVE

To develop analytical skills and problem solving capabilities in the students by educating them in appropriate streams of technology and computational methodologies To develop an interdisciplinary perspective - and - train the students to work with people belonging to different social, cultural, regional and national backgrounds. To develop inter-personal, technical and soft skills in the students and - train them for decision making in real life engineering, managerial and human resource related problems. To inculcate - professional ethics and a sense of responsibility towards physical, social, economic, political and cultural environment. To inculcate the habit and an attitude for life long learning in our students.
